About drift

Drift is pre-deploy testing for multi-agent AI systems. It auto-generates coordination-breaking scenarios from your state's schema, runs them through your agent, and surfaces the crashes plus silent divergences you wouldn't have written a test for because you didn't think of them.

The headline integration is the LangGraph adapter (Adapter tab) — point it at any compiled graph and get findings in 30 seconds. The native simulator (New Run / Runs / Compare) is the original stack that drives drift's own agents end-to-end; runs are written to runs/ as JSONL and can be replayed or compared. The Detect tab benchmarks drift's LLM judge against human-annotated traces from the MAST paper.
